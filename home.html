Our Misson:
To Better Our Community

Bronx Support Groups is a nonprofit app/website service that connects the residents of the Bronx to addiction support groups around the borough. Currently, Bronx residents are at a disadvantage when it comes to accessing addiction support groups close to them: the programs exist but there is not an easy way to access them. This service serves as a solution to this problem.
